The Neighbour Discovery Protocol (NDP) is [[IPv6|IPv6's]] method of [[ARP]]. It resolves [[Studies/MAC|MAC Addresses]] into IPv6 Addresses.

NDP uses IPv6 [[Link-local Addresses]] for addressing its packets.
